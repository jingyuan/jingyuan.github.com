Chinese Classical Garden

This is a task aiming to gain more knowledge and better understanding of the Chinese Classical Garden. With only a rectangle as the site without any context, students can set the master/owner by themselves and design with a theme or inspiration from the ancient culture. So to say there is only cultural context but without a physical one (It is also a common phenomenon that Chinese gardens are quite separated from the context and aiming to create a different ideal place for people live in the city). It is very interesting to get into the culture and learn to design in a classical way.
