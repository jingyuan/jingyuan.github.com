  • Perspective of the waterfront
    The project tries to create a bond out of the boundary. Originally the right side of the river is a newly build housing community while the left side is the isolated old town.

  • Analysis
    Site is located at one of the zones of unprecedented developing Shanghai. It is a part of the old town which is left there with a lot of problems left by time. A lot of old houses remained demolished and immigrations poured in with security issues. Boundaries have generated and the site is now isolated due to the development of its outskirt.

  • Concept
    The boundaries have been generated in a long term. We hope the situation will change in a natural process by providing chances of meeting and communicating, bringing the society together again and restoring the old town at the same time. As the final stage, the boundary will turn into a bond.

  • Current situation
    By using the diagram, problems are illustrated and sorted. There is also potential out of the problems, like demolished buildings with a history and a lot of stories and vacant lots with possibilities for activity.

  • Activities
    The current activity and ones that are possible to introduce to the site are analyzed and mapped. Connections are also pointed out on the map.

  • Plan
    A system of space and activity is generated. Basically the system includes three nodes, a bird and flower market, a craft and antique market and a food market, which all based on the culture and needs of the community. Three big nodes are linked by paths and small nodes.

  • Structure
    The whole structure of the system, consisting of core, which is the main market, point, which is the small activity nodes and connections. The illustration is showing the one of the three nodes, the food market.

  • Section of the West Gate Stree
    Section is showing the space of the main street and a branch. The aim is to bring the quality to the place with small interventions.

  • Perspective of the main street
    The space is welcoming and ready for creating communication. People will be able to stop, sit and meet others in the neighborhood. Small business will grow along two sides.

  • Section of small nodes
    Some enlarged space in between the dwellers are used as small nodes for daily activities. More chances to meet neighbors.

  • Perspective of a medium node
    As a welcoming sports that doesn't take much space, badminton is brought into the project. This kind of group sports will bring people together and create a social atmosphere.

Activated Bond

The whole idea for this old town is to make it a real society but not commercial. Another example, the East Gate street duplicated the commercial way and turned out to be a mediocre shopping place, which is not welcoming at all. The West Gate has been left over, it remained its original pattern and has been developing in a natural way though with a lot of problems. The basic methodology is to introduce a system to motivate people and bring social life to the place and eventually break the boundary and make it into the bond between different communities. Though A physical boundary can generate a emotional one, we believe that emotional bond can really help break the physical one.
