Kindergarten Design

The site is in a small community. The kindergarten is suppose to serve the nearby residents.

The surrounding residential area is quiet peaceful without too many cars passing by. There are requirements and restrictions for the design of the kindergarten: There should be 6 classes in this kindergarten, accommodating about 124 children. Each class should have an activity room, a bedroom, a washroom and a storage. An activity space is also needed for all. As for teachers and logistics, there should be an office, a meeting room, a kitchen, a clinic, a washroom, a storage and a carpentry. Due to these restrictions and requirements , and the specialty of the users--children, the design stared after a thorough study and understanding of the behavior and preference of kids from 2-6, especially focused on the impact of the body scale and preference of the color and shape.

Finally, toy bricks was chosen to be the base geometry and reference to generate the design, hoping that the whole kindergarten can be fun from all aspects for children.
