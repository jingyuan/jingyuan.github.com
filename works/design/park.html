  • Analysis
    The site is located in the important industrial zone in Shanghai. It is now in the transformation from the left over by the industry into a park. With a good location close to the subway station, this whole area is going to be an important hub.

  • Analysis
    The specific site is a part of the hub. Though close to the rive, there is a-6.9m-dam in between. The whole idea is to take use of the dam and integrate it into the area.

  • Plan
    The dyke is designed as a part of the park. Due to the height of the dam, it is also combined with green to make the whole structure softer and more integrated. The left over cargos are also used to create space and light installation. Playground is included as well.

  • Section and devision
    The section shows the functional and spatial devision of the park. The northern part is more open while the southern part is more nature and closer. The cargo will be the element that connect the both sides.

  • Installation
    The left over cargo is an important element that used in the whole park. Cargos will be with different height with some partly buried in the ground. They will help create interesting space and provide light installation during the night.

  • Night perspective
    The view on the main road that connect the east and west with the light from the cargo, aiming to provide a safe and nice environment for people live nearby and staff working overtime in the hub.

Park Design

The whole area is left by the former industrial activity that is now in a transformation into an important hub. The site is a part of the area and is aiming to provide activity space and open space for the surround working offices and residents. The whole design is to reduce the affection of the high dyke and make it integrated with the whole park and acting as a connection along the rive rather than a big boundary. The park is roughly divided into two parts, with northern part open and the south closer and quiet. The left over cargos are also taken care of and become part of the park, creating interesting spaces and providing a safe and nice route for home.
